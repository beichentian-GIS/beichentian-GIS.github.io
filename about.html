About Me

Hi! My name is Beichen!

I am the creator of the Little Map House.

I am a geospatial analyst, an emerging cartographer, and an amateur planner.

I create each map with a unique personality to display their messages and information.

I am currently a GIS certificate student with a previous Master's degree in urban and regional planning (environmental planning and GIS emphases). My main research interests include but are not limited to habitat sustainability modeling, infectious disease mapping, user interface (UI) design, so on and so forth. In my spare time, I like jogging along Lake Mendota, makingand, and watching inspiring movies (e.g.,,). You are welcome to bug me and share me anything you like!
